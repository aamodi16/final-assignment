About Us

Welcome to our world of natural beauty and timeless design. At Wooden Decor, we believe in turning simple pieces of wood into heartfelt works of art. Each product is thoughtfully handcrafted to bring warmth, elegance, and character to your space.

The soul behind this creative venture is Vibha Jadhav, a passionate artist who finds joy in crafting unique pieces from wood. What began as a hobby in her home workshop has grown into a brand that reflects creativity, sustainability, and love for traditional craftsmanship.

Whether it's rustic wall art, cozy kitchen decor, or custom-made gifts, every item in our collection is made with love, care, and attention to detail.
